Pawan Kalyan Konidela

Politician & Actor

Deputy Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh

About

Pawan Kalyan is popularly known as Power Star. He belongs to the Konidela family and has a massive youth fan base. Apart from cinema, he is known for impactful public speeches and social movements.

Career

Film Career

Debuted in 1996. Major hits include Tholi Prema, Thammudu, Kushi, Jalsa, Gabbar Singh and Attarintiki Daredi.

Political Career

Founded Jana Sena Party in 2014. Serving as Deputy CM of Andhra Pradesh since June 2024.
